About PIV Installer NI

Northern Ireland's local ventilation experts — curing condensation and mould since 2018

Who We Are

Belfast's Trusted PIV Experts

PIV Installer NI is a specialized ventilation company based in Belfast. We focus exclusively on Positive Input Ventilation (PIV)—the permanent, scientific cure for home dampness, condensation, and toxic black mould.

Founded in 2018, we set out with a clear purpose: to offer a lasting solution to the severe moisture problems that plague Northern Ireland's housing stock. Condensation and damp are not just cosmetic problems—they damage building materials and aggravate chronic respiratory issues like asthma.

Since our inception, we have completed over 1,000 installations across Greater Belfast and surrounding towns. We are built on honest advice, high-quality workmanship, and real, verifiable results.

We are a local, independent company, not a national call centre. When you contact us, you speak directly with local technicians who understand the specific damp issues of properties in Belfast, Lisburn, Derry, and beyond.
