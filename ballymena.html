Curing condensation across Ballymena

Known as the "City of the Seven Towers", Ballymena is the hub of mid-Antrim — a busy market town surrounded by countryside and villages. From the older terraces around Harryville and the town centre to the larger estates at Ballykeel and Dunclug and the homes of Broughshane and Galgorm, damp winters mean condensation and black mould are a familiar problem.

We install positive input ventilation (PIV) throughout Ballymena and the BT42 and BT43 postcodes, as well as Broughshane, Cullybackey, Ahoghill, Galgorm, Kells and Portglenone. With most homes having loft access, a quiet Drimaster loft unit is usually the ideal whole-home solution.

Fitted in around two hours with no mess, PIV replaces damp stale air with fresh filtered air. Most Ballymena customers see windows running dry within a week.
