Curing condensation in the North West

Derry / Londonderry sits on the banks of the River Foyle, and the city's mix of older Cityside terraces, Waterside homes and outlying estates regularly battles high humidity, condensation and persistent black mould — particularly through the long, wet North West winters.

We install positive input ventilation (PIV) throughout Derry / Londonderry and the BT47 and BT48 postcodes, covering the Cityside, the Waterside, Culmore, Strathfoyle, Newbuildings and the surrounding villages. Whether it's a period terrace or a modern estate home, PIV is the proven permanent fix.

One quiet unit, fitted in around two hours, gently floods the whole home with fresh filtered air and pushes the damp out. Most North West customers see windows running dry within a week.
