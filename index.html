The Problem

Is damp and mould ruining your home?

Northern Ireland's wet climate makes condensation and mould a major structural and health issue. Traditional venting methods are often ineffective. Typical symptoms include:

  • Morning Condensation: Heavy water droplets running down windows.
  • Black Mould: Spreading in wall corners, window frames, and ceilings.
  • Musty Odours: Stale, damp air trapped in wardrobes, fabrics, and carpets.
  • Ruined Redecorating: Repainted walls peeling or growing mould again in weeks.
  • Allergies & Asthma: Breathing issues aggravated by mould spores and dust mites.
The Solution

Positive Input Ventilation (PIV)

PIV is the UK's most popular permanent cure. It tackles the root cause by displacing moisture-laden air rather than simply covering up the damp symptoms.

  • Cures Mould & Condensation: Stops them from forming by diluting humidity.
  • Continuously Filters Air: Pushes fresh, clean air throughout your home 24/7.
  • Low Running Cost: Consumes only 10W–15W, costing about £12–£15 a year.
  • Fast 2-Hour Installation: Fitted quickly with zero structural disruption.
  • Landlord & HMO Compliant: Satisfies Building Regulations Part F (NI).

Spot the Signs

Signs your home needs better ventilation

Many homeowners don't realise they have a ventilation problem until the damage is done. If you recognise any of these, PIV will help.

Streaming windows

Water droplets forming on glass and sills every morning that you have to wipe down.

Black mould spots

Dark patches creeping across ceilings, wall corners, window seals, or behind furniture.

Musty, stale smell

A damp odour that lingers in wardrobes, soft furnishings, and rooms even after airing.

Worse allergies & asthma

Sneezing, congestion, or disrupted sleep aggravated by mould spores and dust mites.

Damp clothes & fabrics

Clothing, bedding, or curtains that feel cold and damp to the touch.

Peeling paint & wallpaper

Freshly decorated walls bubbling, peeling, or growing mould again within weeks.

Benefits

Why Positive Input Ventilation is the best choice

Healthier Indoor Air

Filters out pollen, dust mites, and outdoor allergens while continuously diluting indoor volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and stale CO2.

Incredibly Low Running Cost

Ultra-efficient DC motors mean the system consumes less power than a low-energy LED bulb. Significantly cheaper than running a noisy, heavy dehumidifier.

Protects Property Structure

Stops water from collecting on timber joints, drywall, and plaster, preventing long-term timber rot and wallpaper peeling.

Near-Zero Maintenance

Install and forget. The robust medical-grade filters inside the unit only need to be replaced once every 4 to 5 years.
