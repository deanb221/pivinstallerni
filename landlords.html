The Risk

Mould complaints are a landlord's biggest headache

Condensation and black mould are the number one cause of disputes between tenants and landlords in Northern Ireland. Left unaddressed, they cost you far more than the fix:

  • Repeat complaints: Wiping mould and repainting never lasts — it returns within weeks.
  • Disrepair claims: Tenants increasingly raise formal complaints over damp and mould.
  • Property damage: Damp rots timber, ruins plaster, and devalues your asset.
  • Void periods: Mouldy properties are harder to let and command lower rents.
  • Tenant health: Duty of care for vulnerable tenants, children, and asthma sufferers.
The Fix

One permanent, low-cost solution

A single PIV unit cures whole-home condensation and mould at the source — and keeps working with zero tenant effort and near-zero running cost.

  • Stops mould returning: Keeps humidity below the level mould needs to grow.
  • Tenant-proof: Fit and forget — no reliance on tenants opening windows.
  • Pennies to run: Around £10–£15 a year, far cheaper than dehumidifiers.
  • Supports compliance: Aligns with Building Regulations Part F (NI) ventilation guidance.
  • Documented: EIC electrical certificate issued for your records on every install.
